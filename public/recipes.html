BAKED BRIE

Top whole brie with crushed garlic, then chopped, drained sun-dried tomatoes (in oil). Bake 350°F til cheese starts to melt. Garnish with parsley. Serve with crackers.

THE CHEESE BALL ★ ♥

INGREDIENTS

  • 8oz cream cheese, room temp
  • 2 cups shredded sharp cheddar cheese
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1/2 cup chopped walnuts
  • 1/2 packet dry onion soup mix
  • 1/2 cup crushed crackers


  • 1/2 cup chopped walnuts

INSTRUCTIONS

Beat cream cheese, cheddar, and sour cream until smooth. Mix in nuts, onion soup and cracker crumbs.

Shape into ball. Roll in chopped walnuts.

Makes about 4 cups
If too soft to shape into ball, chill it for a while.
GRANDMA'S ZUCCHINI SALSA ★

INGREDIENTS

  • 4 cups zucchini, chopped
  • 1 cup onion, chopped
  • 1/2 red pepper, chopped
  • 1/2 green pepper, chopped
  • 1 jalapeno (or to taste), chopped
  • 1 tbsp salt


  • 2 cups chopped tomatoes
  • 5.5 oz. can tomato pasta
  • 1/2 tsp dry mustard
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp cumin
  • 1/2 cup white vinegar
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 tsp chili powder
  • 2 tsp cornstarch

INSTRUCTIONS

Put zucchini, onion, red & green pepper, jalapeno, and salt in dutch oven. Mix together and leave in refrigerator overnight.

Next morning, DON'T DRAIN! Add the rest of the ingredients and mix well. Boil for 10 minutes, then simmer for an hour. Chill. Makes about 1 litre.



If you want to make enough for multiple jars (and you know how to jar food safely), quadruple the recipe.
HERB MARINATED MOZZARELLA

INGREDIENTS

  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • 1/2 red onion, finely diced
  • 1 tbsp chopped fresh basil
  • 2 tbsp chopped fresh chives
  • 1 tbsp chopped fresh thyme
  • 1 tbsp chopped fresh parsley
  • salt & pepper
(if using dried herbs, reduce amounts to teaspoonfuls)

INSTRUCTIONS

Stir together herbs, oil and onion along with salt and pepper. Add cheese and stir to coat. Cover and let stand at room temperature 1-3 hours. Place on serving tray and serve with crackers or breads.

POPPYCOCK ★

INGREDIENTS

  • 24 cups fresh popped popcorn (unsalted, unbuttered)
  • 2 2/3 cups brown sugar
  • 1 1/2 cup margarine
  • 1 cup corn syrup
  • 1 tsp vanilla (or more if you like)
  • nuts, roasted & unsalted - can be almonds, pecans, hazelnuts, and/or brazils (NOT walnuts!)
  • Optional: some people add cherries, raisins, or craisins
    • This recipe is very customizable. Add whatever your heart desires

INSTRUCTIONS

(This is much easier to make with 2 people)

Spray big stainless steel bowl with Pam to avoid sticking. Place popcorn & nuts in bowl.

Combine sugar, corn syrup, and margarine in large pan over low heat. Stir well while it melts together, slowly raising the heat until it forms a hard ball. Remove from heat and add vanilla and stir very well.

Pour over popcorn and nuts and mix well. Spread over greased baking sheets. Let cool and dry.

Makes about 3 ice cream pails full.

KRUMPRA & KNADLE ★

INGREDIENTS

  • 6 potatoes, cut into small pieces (not too small, or they'll turn to mush)
  • onion, to taste
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • paprika, to taste
  • water


  • 2 cups flour
  • salt & pepper
  • water


  • 1/2 cup shredded medium cheddar
  • 1/2 cup plain yogurt

IPREN

(potato soup)

Sauté onions in 2 tablespoons of butter. Sprinkle with paprika, and add enough water to cover potatoes.

Season with salt, and boil until potatoes are tender.

KNADLES

(bread dumplings)

Combine flour, salt & pepper, and enough water to make hard dough. Mix well.

Flatten pieces of dough. They should be about as wide as a loonie (approx. 1") and about 1/8" thick.

When potato soup is ready, "pluck" knadle dough into soup. Cook 10-15 minutes. Add cheddar and yogurt. Eat! Enjoy!

BANANA CHOCOLATE CHIP MUFFINS ★ ♥

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 3/4 cups flour
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 3 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup chocolate chips, or to taste


  • 1 egg
  • 1/4 cup cooking oil
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 3 overripe mashed bananas

INSTRUCTIONS

Combine first 5 dry ingredients into large bowl. Mix and make a well in the center.

Beat egg until frothy. Mix in oil, milk and bananas. Pour into well. Stir only until moist - batter will still be lumpy. Fill muffin cups 3/4 full. Bake at 400°F for 20-25 minutes.

makes about 1 dozen muffins
CHOCOLATE BANANA CAKE

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 cup unsalted butter
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 3-4 very ripe bananas, mashed
  • 1/3 cup sour cream
  • 2 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 2 1/4 cup flour
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/3 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 cup boiling water

INSTRUCTIONS

Add baking soda to sour cream; let foam while making rest of cake

Cream sugar & butter. Add eggs & bananas. Beat. Add sour cream. Mix salt, cocoa, and water. Mix in flour. Add vanilla. Mix.

Pour into greased 9" x 13" pan, or bundt pan. Bake at 350°F for about 1 hour.

goes well with cream cheese icing
THE MOST SOFT & CHEWY CHOCOLATE CHIP COOKIES ♥

INGREDIENTS

  • 3/4 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tsp vanilla
  • 2 cups flour
  • 2 tsp cornstarch
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 cup chocolate chips (or to taste)

INSTRUCTIONS

Beat butter and both sugars together until creamy (approx. 2 minutes). Beat in egg and vanilla.

In separate bowl whisk flour, cornstarch, baking soda, and salt. Add to wet ingredients, beating until combined. Fold in the chocolate chips.

CHILL THE DOUGH!!! For at least 1 hour in the fridge, but preferably 3 or more. You can leave it to chill for up to 72 hours, but make sure the dough is covered.

Preheat oven 350°F. Line baking sheets with parchment paper.

Roll dough into 1 heaping tablespoon balls. Place each 3" apart on baking sheets.

Bake for 11-12 minutes, until barely golden on edges. They will look underbaked on top. This is normal.

Cool cookies on baking sheet for 5 minutes, then transfer to cooling rack.

makes about 30 cookies. stays fresh for about a week if kept covered at room temp
TOO MANY BRAN MUFFINS ★

INGREDIENTS

  • 4 cups bran flakes cereal
  • 2 cups all bran cereal
  • 2 cups boiling water


  • 1 cup butter
  • 1 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 4 eggs
  • 4 cups buttermilk


  • 5 cups flour
  • 2 tbsp baking soda
  • 1 tbsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 cups raisins (optional)

INSTRUCTIONS

In large bowl, combine cereals and boiling water. Let stand.

In separate bowl cream butter and sugars. Beat in eggs one at a time. Mix in buttermilk. Stir in cereal mix.

In another bowl combine flour, soda, powder, salt, and raisins. Mix well. Add to batter, and stir to combine. Store in fridge.

Fill muffin cups 3/4 full. Bake at 400°F for 20-25 minutes. Remove from pan after 5 minutes.

Makes a LOT of muffins. I'm not kidding. You might wanna keep the batter covered in the fridge and bake in small batches. The batter should keep for about 6 weeks.
If you choose to bake them all at once, be prepared to gift some to your friends and loved ones. Or at least put some in the freezer.
