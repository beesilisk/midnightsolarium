HERB MARINATED MOZZARELLA
  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • 1/2 red onion, finely diced
  • 1 tbsp chopped fresh basil
  • 2 tbsp chopped fresh chives
  • 1 tbsp chopped fresh thyme
  • 1 tbsp chopped fresh parsley
  • salt & pepper
    • (if using dried herbs, reduce amounts to teaspoonfuls)

Stir together herbs, oil and onion along with salt and pepper. Add cheese and stir to coat. Cover and let stand at room temperature 1-3 hours. Place on serving tray and serve with crackers or breads.

PEACH SALSA
  • 8 ripe peaches, coarsely chopped
  • 1 small red pepper, cut into 1/4" diced pieces
  • 2 green onions, chopped
  • 1/4 cup peach or apricot jam
  • 2 tbsp balsamic vinegar
  • 2 tbsp olive oil

In a large bowl, toss ingredients until combined. Cover and refrigerate until ready to serve up, or 2 days in fridge.
