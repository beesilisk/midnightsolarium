what is wilde.AI?

Welcome to wilde.AI, the large language model that’s revolutionizing the workflow of artistic creation.

In the past, the only people capable of creating valuable art were the privileged and naturally gifted. Those individuals would spend entire lifetimes perfecting their craft – time that would be better spent on more valuable forms of labour such as programming and day trading.

Now, wilde.ai lets anyone create poetry – no logins, subscriptions, or effort required!

Using a curated dataset containing the best works of talented poets, wilde.ai has turned the unenjoyable process of creating art into an instantly rewarding clickable button. By excising the creative process, wilde.AI is able to maximize the pleasure that results from artistic endeavours without requiring genuine devotion.

To put it simply: wilde.AI is the only tool you’ll ever need or want in order to craft great poetry.
