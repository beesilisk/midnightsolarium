welcome!

You've reached a website dedicated to things that grow in the night! Visitors are invited to pop in and unwind, look around, or just take a quick breather before venturing further into the world wide web.

Midnight Solarium is the personal website / ongoing pet project of an easily overstimulated bumblebee. Exhausted by the deluge of discontent that is the modern web, Bee (that's me!) hopes to create a space where they can nurture the things they love.
